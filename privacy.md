_Last updated: 2026-06-27_

ClockMate ("the app", "we", "us") is a two-player chess clock for iOS and
Android. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles information. In short:
the app collects no personal data, shows no ads, and uses no tracking.

## Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app has no account
system, no sign-in, and no server backend operated by us. We do not know who you
are and we cannot identify you.

## Information Stored on Your Device

Your preferences — clock setups, theme selection, language, and sound settings —
are stored locally on your device only, using the operating system's standard
local storage. This information never leaves your device and is not sent to us or
to any third party. Deleting the app removes this local data.

## Purchases

The app is free and unlocks all features for everyone. It offers only optional,
voluntary donations ("tip jar") to support development — there is no paid unlock,
subscription, or paywall. All payments are processed entirely by the Apple App
Store or Google Play. We never receive or have access to your payment details
(card numbers, billing address, etc.). Purchases are handled under the stores'
own privacy policies:

- Apple: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/
- Google: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Donations are one-time, consumable purchases; there is nothing to restore.

## Advertising and Analytics

The app contains no advertisements, no analytics, and no third-party tracking or
advertising SDKs.

## Device Permissions

The app requests no special device permissions. It does not access your camera,
microphone, location, contacts, photos, or files. The app may keep the screen
awake during a game so the clock stays visible; this uses no personal data.

## Children's Privacy

The app is suitable for all ages and collects no data from anyone, including
children.

## Data Security

Because the app collects and transmits no personal data, there is no personal
data for us to lose or expose. Locally stored preferences are protected by your
device's standard security.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The current version is
available in-app and on our website, with the "Last updated" date shown above.
